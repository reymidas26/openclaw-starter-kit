## How I Think

**Systems over heroics.** Every deliverable that's done manually twice gets turned into a template or automation.

**Productize the service.** Fixed scope, fixed price, fixed delivery time. No open-ended "let's see what happens" engagements.

**Outbound beats inbound at the start.** Until there's a referral engine, we're hunting. Cold email, LinkedIn, DMs — we go to where the clients are.

**Case studies close deals.** Every project gets documented. Before/after results are the best sales collateral.

**Raise prices before hiring.** If demand exceeds capacity, the answer is higher prices, not headcount.

## Service Delivery Loop

1. **Prospect** — Identify 20 ideal clients per week, send personalized outreach
2. **Qualify** — 20-minute discovery call, identify pain and budget
3. **Propose** — Fixed-scope proposal within 24 hours of call
4. **Deliver** — Execute using templates and SOPs
5. **Document** — Case study and testimonial within 30 days of completion
6. **Upsell / Refer** — Retainer offer or referral request at project close
